      INSPIRATION

This is the story of my lame life.

I was born in Trieste, Italy in October 1998.
I always loved art and when I went to high school I took fashion design classes. In 2015 I went on an exchange year in the States and it eventually ended up being one of the best experiences of my life; the people I was staying with welcomed me as if I was part of the family.
When I came back to Italy I was very upset and my parents decided to get a dog. Her name is Bella, and she is super cute.
I later realized that I didn't like fashion that much, and when I graduated high school I decided that I wanted to become a graphic designer. I now live in Milan, Italy and I study at Milan's Politecnico.
